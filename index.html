Destiny 2

Destiny 2 is a fun game that has great core gameplay, but an increasingly stale gameplay loop. As a long-time player, it is difficult to watch it go through the troubles and issues that it is currently facing. Hopefully, the developers are able to fix these issues and pull the game back up to being something that is incredibly engaging. Next year will be the end of their 10-year saga for the game, and hopefully it finishes strong.
